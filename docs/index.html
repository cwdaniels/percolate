Shared checklists

Pages

Wiki-style notes anyone can edit — recipes, how-tos, opening routines. Write - [ ] in front of a line and it becomes a real checkbox, right there on the page.

Roast

Built around how the roaster actually works, not around how software usually works.

🫘

Bean library

Every bean on file — origin, roast level, flavor notes, certifications. Sort it, search it, let the whole team keep it current.

🔥 Roasting orders

Paste a whole batch at once — each store's name on its own line becomes its own order, with its beans listed underneath.

Check the store's name and every bean checks with it. Uncheck it and it all comes back. Tap Edit to fix a typo, bold a name, indent a line, or drop a bean — right on the list, no starting over.

When it's out the door, whoever's driving taps Delivered right on the order card — no separate list, no second app. It logs who dropped it off and when, then settles quietly into the Delivered history.

To Roast → Ready to Deliver → Delivered

Finished orders don't disappear — they wait in Delivered for whoever needs the paperwork.
